Dear Sir / Madam,

We THANK YOU for your interest in knowing about our Society Membership details.

The Objective of our society is to provide Premier housing facilities (To acquire and develop lands for residential layouts, Villa Plots, construction of row houses & apartments) for IT employees, Engineers, Diplomas and all sectors involving IT and Engineers. Currently, we are on a membership drive & request you to make use of this opportunity to become life members of our society.

The Main Advantages Of Society Membership Are As Follows :

  • Upon Payment of necessary membership fees, each applicant would be considered as Society Members for life and will be allotted 2 Shares of Rupees 1000/ each.
  • Members will be given Identity cards and Share Certificates.
  • Members will be eligible to apply for plots in any or all of the Society layouts at a discount rate. (Marginally lesser than the normal pricing system).
  • Members will play an active role in the functioning of the society.
  • Members will be eligible for a certain dividend / gifts upon successful completion of each project and milestones.
  • Members are eligible to refer interested people to book/buy plots in society layouts and would earn a token of appreciation and a nominal referral bonus.
  • Members will receive regular updates / newsletters on the Project launches, Project progress Updates and general information about society.
  • Membership fee / Share amount will be a refundable amount, at any point of time in case the members wish to surrender their membership. They may do so by surrendering the Share Certificate and Identity Cards in a prescribed format.

Membership Fees

As a onetime membership fees, an amount of Rupees Two Thousand Three Hundred only (Rupees 2300/-) would be charged during membership registration process.
  • Share Amount = Rupees 2000/- Only (Rupees 1000/- each X 2 Shares).
  • Administrative and application charges = Rupees 300/- only.

Eligibility

We require the following documents for the registration of your membership
  • IT or allied Company Identity card / Engineering Certificate in any discipline and cadre. (M. Tech / B. Tech / Diploma / BE )
  • 3 passport Photographs.
  • A Crossed Cheque for Rupees 2300/- only.

Please feel free to reach us in case you need any clarifications or additional details.

Thanking you,

Management Team

Why Housing society

How it works?

Every person would like to own a house of their own. With apartments becoming a hassle free and safer method of owning a house, most people, especially those who are not native to Bengaluru, opt for apartments over independent houses.

With purchasing independent houses comes a lot of questions of land ownership irregularities, price points, responsibility of following through with people which adds on to existing responsibilities and finding a trustworthy mode to conduct these transactions become a hassle.

Societies are regulated by the Government and have set guidelines and by-laws to follow. Your investment in the purchase becomes safe and hassle free. With Society, you are presented with an opportunity like no other- housing options with 15% to 25% less than the current market.



How do you benefit?

  • At Society, we conceptualize, plan execute and deliver your property.
  • We handle everything from land analysis, layout planning backgrounds check affordability of our society members, quality checks up until delivery. Our Society Members are offered a range of options from plots, row houses, apartments or villas.
  • An opportunity to become members with first right to any project launched by the society which will be provided to its members only, at affordable and fair market value pricing, typically 15% to 25% lower than the market price, maximum mitigation in escalation of costs, bulk requirements, etc benefitting the Members.
  • Society, backed not just by Government bodies, but also has a board of directors that comprise of current and former employees from leading IT & ITES firms such as Infosys, HCL, WIPRO etc, who come with the experience of over three decades and have mentored people from all walks of life.
  • Society along with its board of Directors came together to offer every IT & ITES Employees incomparable housing options at affordable pricing. Society also plans to streamline the process of owning a land in Karnataka as well smoothen the process for people in the IT & ITES industry in Karnataka. The intent is to make affordable but premium housing available to every person working in the IT and ITES sector in the years to come.
  • A society where every employee of IT and ITES can call their own and put across their needs not only for Bengaluru but outside Bengaluru locations in Karnataka, while the Directors and the office bearers of the Society will put their best effort to make it possible for the Members.
  • A lot of effort has gone and work done by its current directors for the last 3.5 years to make this a reality. Become a member and take complete advantage of the opportunity for the overall benefit of IT and ITES employee’s, ex-employees and their families.

We help you find your economical home

We constantly strive in uniting IT Employees for a safer better & healthier life.

We believe that everyone should have a home they need at the price they can afford.


Real Estate prices sometimes feel a little unrealistic especially in Bengaluru which has a large migrating population coming in year after a year. Bengaluru being the Silicon Valley of India has a large population of IT & ITES Employees who moved to the city and have been constantly moving around the city in search of better housing options.

The founders of Society along with the board of directors, who come with over 20 years of experience in both real estate and IT industry respectively, decided to start on a housing society where they could help fellow colleagues to find a permanent solution to their woes.

Society provides housing options for anyone, who has worked in the IT & ITES sector and is considering settling in Karnataka. Society is backed not just by government bodies, but also has a board of directors that comprise of current and former Employees from leading IT & ITES firms.

The profile of the Directors comprises of VPs and SVPs from top IT companies and MNCs like, Infosys, HCL, IBM, HP, Atos, etc. to name a few. All the employees and ex-employees of IT and ITES sector can benefit from this government regulated social enterprise.
